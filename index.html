What We Do

Core Capabilities

Composite Structures Expertise

Deep technical knowledge of composite manufacturing processes, material specifications, and quality requirements developed through direct industry experience supporting SpaceX, Boeing, and military rotorcraft programs.

Aerospace Component Supply

Procurement and distribution of composite structures, airframe components, and precision aerospace hardware to government and defense end users through vetted domestic manufacturing networks.

Supplier Development

Active development of qualified U.S. small business manufacturer relationships across the aerospace composites and precision hardware sectors to support scalable, compliant contract fulfillment.

NDI Inspection Coordination

End-to-end coordination of non-destructive inspection services for composite aerospace structures — including logistics, Nadcap-certified lab sourcing, chain of custody, and full documentation management.

Supply Chain Logistics

Coordination of specialized aerospace freight, packaging compliance, and delivery management for high-value government contract items — including chain of custody documentation and receiving verification.

Government Contract Management

Prime contractor services on federal supply contracts pursued through DLA DIBBS and SAM.gov, serving agencies including DLA, NAVAIR, AFWERX, and other DoD and federal acquisition channels. Full compliance management including FAR requirements, quality documentation, and delivery coordination.

About UpMarkAero

Charleston-Based.
Technically Credible.
Aerospace Focused.

UpMarkAero is the trade name of UpMarkDefense LLC, a South Carolina limited liability company established to serve the aerospace and defense supply chain as a small business prime contractor and supplier.

Founded by a mechanical engineer with hands-on composite manufacturing experience across SpaceX launch vehicle structures, Boeing commercial airframe programs, and U.S. military rotorcraft — UpMarkAero understands the material specifications, process requirements, and quality standards your parts actually need to meet.

We are actively seeking qualified U.S. manufacturers across the aerospace composites and precision hardware sectors to establish long-term supplier partnerships supporting federal contract fulfillment.
